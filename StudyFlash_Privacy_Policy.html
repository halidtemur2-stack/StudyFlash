Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

StudyFlash ("the site," "we," "our") is a free tool that helps students create, study, and share flashcards, slideshows, and quizzes. This page explains what information is collected when you use the site and how it's used.

1. Information Stored in Your Browser

StudyFlash saves the flashcard decks you create directly in your web browser's local storage (a feature built into all modern browsers). This means:

  • Your decks stay on your own device and are not uploaded to any server we control.
  • We do not have access to the content of your flashcards.
  • Clearing your browser's data/cache will delete your saved decks.
  • If you use the "Share/Import" feature, the deck content is encoded into a text code or downloadable file that you choose to send to someone else — we do not see or store that data either.

2. Advertising

StudyFlash is free to use and is partly supported by advertising. We work with a third-party advertising network (Adsterra) to display ads, including a short ad that may be shown before unlocking the "Easier Words" simplified slideshow feature.

  • Our advertising partner may use cookies, device identifiers, or similar technologies to serve ads and measure ad performance.
  • These third-party ad providers may collect information such as your IP address, browser type, and general usage patterns, in accordance with their own privacy policies.
  • We do not control the specific ads shown or the data collection practices of our advertising partner. You can review Adsterra's own privacy practices at their website.
  • We do not require or encourage anyone to click on ads — the ad-supported feature only requires watching/viewing the ad for its duration.

3. Cookies

Our advertising partner may place cookies in your browser to help deliver and measure ads. You can control or delete cookies through your browser's privacy settings at any time. Disabling cookies may affect ad delivery but will not affect your ability to use the flashcard, slideshow, and quiz features of the site.

4. Children's Privacy

StudyFlash is designed to be used by school students. We do not knowingly collect personal information (such as names, emails, or school details) from any user, including children. All flashcard content stays on the user's own device. If you are a parent or teacher with concerns about a student's use of this site, please reach out using the contact information below.

5. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require account creation, sign-up, or login.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or school information.
  • We do not sell or share personal data, because we do not collect any in the first place.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the site changes. Updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how StudyFlash works, please reach out via the GitHub repository for this project.
